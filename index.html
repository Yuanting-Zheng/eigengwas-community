Research Directions

Core Research — AI for Science

Computational methods and AI as engines, accelerating the translation from genomic data to discovery

EigenGWAS — Genomic Selection Scanning

AI-Driven Detection of Natural Selection Signals

Original unsupervised genomic scanning method based on eigenvector decomposition — detects natural selection signals without requiring population labels. Evolved from a methodological paper into a global online platform, widely applied in human population genetics, crop domestication, and animal genetic diversity research.

Population GeneticsNatural SelectionCrop BreedingOpen Platform
Platform Paper

UK BioCoin — Privacy-Preserving Biobank Analytics

Breaking the Permission Wall & Computation Wall

Achieve user-defined covariate-adjusted trait-specific summary statistics regression without accessing individual-level data. 70-80x computational speedup — a new paradigm for privacy-preserving data sharing in precision medicine.

UK BiobankPrecision MedicinePrivacy-PreservingData Science
GitHub Nat Comm

Genomic Prediction — AI-Powered Smart Breeding

From Statistical Frontiers to Agricultural Productivity

Novel genomic prediction combining Haseman-Elston regression with APY algorithm for ultra-large-scale genomic data. Translating cutting-edge statistical methods into real productivity for crop and livestock genetic improvement.

Smart BreedingGenomic SelectionIndustrial TranslationHE-APY
Crop J 2022

LD Atlas — Cross-Species Linkage Disequilibrium

Genomic Infrastructure for GWAS & Heritability

Building a cross-species LD reference atlas — critical infrastructure for GWAS, heritability estimation, and polygenic score computation. Enabling efficient estimation of large-scale LD patterns across diverse species.

Linkage DisequilibriumPopulation GeneticsData InfrastructureCross-Species
Genome Biol 2025

Clinical Genomics — Precision Medicine

From Bench to Bedside via Statistical Genetics

Applying statistical genetics to dissect the genetic architecture of gestational complications (GDM, ICP), inflammatory bowel disease, and COVID-19 vaccine immune response — driving genomic discoveries toward clinical translation.

Medical Cohort — Data Sharing Infrastructure

Privacy-Preserving Cross-Institution Collaboration

Exploring large-scale medical cohort building and sharing models, with encrypted genotype regression for cross-cohort relative search — enabling multi-center collaboration with data interoperability and privacy protection.
